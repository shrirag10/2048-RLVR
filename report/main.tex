\begin{abstract}
This paper explores the viability of training a Small Language Model (SLM)
to play the puzzle game 2048 using Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable
Rewards (RLVR), specifically Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO).
We benchmark this novel approach against classical RL methods (DQN, PPO)
to determine if LLMs can learn spatial and game reasoning through self-play

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent\textbf{Keywords:} Reinforcement Learning, RLVR, GRPO, 2048,
Deep Q-Network, Proximal Policy Optimization, Language Models
\end{abstract}
